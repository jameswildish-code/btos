How it works

From a watch tap
to an informed decision.

Three steps. No data warehouse to build. No CSV pipeline to maintain. No retraining anyone on a new app.

STEP 01

Connect every source.

One consent flow links wearables, lab panels, genomic reports, epigenetic tests, and medication histories — historical and live.

STEP 02

Reconcile into one record.

We dedupe, time-align, and merge conflicting readings across devices, then expose a single longitudinal record per person.

STEP 03

Act on signal — not noise.

Cohort views, individual flags, and consumer notifications fire from the same rules engine. Same truth, every surface.
