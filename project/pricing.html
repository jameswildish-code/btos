Questions, briefly

Things we get asked
more than once a week.

Do you sell or share member data?

No. Subscription is our only business model. Read our trust page for the audit trail and policies behind every byte.

Does BiotrackOS replace our EHR?

No — and it shouldn’t. We sit beside it, feeding it deduped, FHIR-formatted continuous data via integration.

Is there a free tier for teams?

No, but every plan has a 14-day trial including a guided walkthrough with our onboarding crew. Members on your team always get the free consumer app.

How do you charge insurers and corporates?

Enterprise plans are volume-priced per active member, not per seat. Aggregate-only dashboards never expose individual data.

How long does a team rollout take?

Most customers go live in 2–5 days. The Continuum case study walks through a Friday-to-Monday rollout.
