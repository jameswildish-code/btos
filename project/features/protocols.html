Sub-feature 01

A rule editor
your clinicians can read.

Write protocols in plain language — when a signal, then a recommendation. No code, no spreadsheets, no waiting on engineering. Every rule is versioned, peer-reviewable, and rolls back in one click.

  • If/then editor with biomarker, wearable trend & time conditions
  • Combine signals (AND / OR) across any source
  • Versioned with author, reviewer, and effective date
  • Test against historical cohorts before going live

Sub-feature 03

Clinician in the loop —
or fully automated.

Per protocol, decide who approves. Some recommendations route to a clinician inbox for a one-tap sign-off; others (supplements, lifestyle plans) push straight to the patient. Every decision is logged.

  • Configurable sign-off per protocol — none, any clinician, or specific role
  • One-tap approve / amend / reject with reason
  • Auto-approve thresholds (e.g. supplements only)
  • Full audit trail with patient consent & clinician identity

Sub-feature 04

How it lands
with the person.

The patient sees one card in their app — what changed, why it changed, what their clinician suggests. They accept, ask a question, or decline. The platform tracks adherence and retests on schedule.

  • Explanation card in the consumer app with the underlying signal
  • Accept, ask, or decline — all logged back to the team
  • Auto-reminders, refills, & re-tests scheduled by the protocol
  • Adherence tracked so the rule can self-tune
