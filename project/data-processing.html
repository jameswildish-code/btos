Data processing addendum.

Version 2.4Effective 01 May 2026

This Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") forms part of the Terms of Service between BiotrackOS, Inc. ("Processor") and Customer ("Controller") and reflects the parties' agreement with respect to the processing of Personal Data.

1 — Scope & roles

Controller determines the purposes and means of processing. Processor processes Personal Data only on documented instructions from Controller, including with regard to transfers to third countries.

2 — Subject matter

  • Nature: ingestion, normalisation, storage, and presentation of health data.
  • Duration: term of the Order Form plus 60 days for export.
  • Data subjects: Members enrolled by Controller.
  • Categories: identification data, contact data, health data (special category, Art. 9 GDPR).

4 — Security

Processor implements appropriate technical and organisational measures (TOMs) including: AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit, RBAC + SSO, immutable audit logging, quarterly penetration testing, annual ISO 27001 audit, SOC 2 Type II.

5 — International transfers

Where Personal Data is transferred outside the EEA/UK, Processor relies on the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses (Module 2 — Controller to Processor, 2021/914) and the UK International Data Transfer Addendum, supplemented by the TOMs in §4.

6 — Data subject requests

Processor will assist Controller in responding to data subject requests within 5 business days using technical and organisational measures appropriate to the request.

7 — Breach notification

Processor will notify Controller without undue delay and in any event within 24 hours of becoming aware of a Personal Data Breach affecting Controller's data, with all information required by Art. 33(3) GDPR.

8 — Audit

Processor will make available all information necessary to demonstrate compliance and allow audits, including inspections, conducted by Controller or an auditor mandated by Controller, with reasonable prior notice.

9 — Deletion or return

Upon termination of services, Processor will, at the choice of Controller, delete or return all Personal Data, and delete existing copies unless legally required to retain.

Signing the DPA

The DPA is auto-incorporated into all Order Forms signed after May 1, 2026. To countersign explicitly, email legal@biotrackos.com.
