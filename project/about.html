Company

A company built
so a body can be
understood in one place.

BiotrackOS was started in 2022 by clinicians, athletes, and engineers tired of the same conversation: "Can you bring your numbers next time?" The numbers always existed. The instrument to read them across a life didn't.

We don't believe in another wearable. We believe in the operating system that sits between every wearable, every clinic, and every person — quiet, continuous, and trustworthy enough that you stop thinking about it.

Principles

Four rules we won't break.

01

One record per person.

A body has one history. Our job is to make sure the software treats it that way.

02

Consent is the API.

Nothing leaves a person's record without their permission — granular, revocable, audit-logged.

03

Signal over surveillance.

We build for the data that changes a decision, not the data that scrolls.

04

Clinical-grade, calmly.

HIPAA, SOC 2, ISO 27001 are floors. Quiet, careful design is the ceiling.

05

We make money when our customers do.

No ads, no data resale, no surprise upcharges. Subscription is the only business model we know how to be proud of.
